FAQ

How do I add the widget?

Touch and hold the home or lock screen → “+” / “Customize” → find “Palavri” → choose a style (word + translation, example + translation, or full).

The word isn’t changing?

A new word appears every morning. In the widget, the arrows ◀ ▶ flip through the current word’s facets (example, phrase, collocation).

Does it work offline?

Yes, fully. No internet required.

Translation language

Currently Ukrainian. In Settings you can choose topics, level, and words per day.
