Privacy Policy

Effective: 29 June 2026

Palavri (the “app”) is built with respect for your privacy. The app works fully offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data we collect

None. There are no accounts, no servers, and no registration.

Data on your device

Your progress and favorites are stored only on your device and are never sent anywhere. Deleting the app removes this data.

Notifications

“Word of the day” reminders are scheduled locally on your device and are not sent to any server.

Tracking, analytics, ads

The app uses no analytics, ad networks, trackers, or third-party data-collection SDKs.

Children

The app does not collect data from users of any age.

Changes

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
